About Us

SkyScholars was created to empower the next generation of engineers. Through engaging workshops, hands-on experiments, and mentorship, we help students explore the science of flight and develop critical thinking skills that soar beyond the classroom.

Aerodynamics Basics

Interactive lessons and demos explaining lift, drag, thrust, and flight control principles.

Workshops where students design and build model planes, learning engineering fundamentals.

Mentorship & Exploration

Meet real engineers and pilots, explore career paths, and ask questions during live sessions.

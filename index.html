About Us

SkyScholars empowers the next generation of engineers through engaging workshops, hands-on experiments, and mentorship opportunities. We inspire students to explore the science of flight while fostering creativity, problem-solving, and a passion for STEM.

Our Programs

Aerodynamics Basics

Interactive lessons and demos explaining lift, drag, thrust, and flight control principles.

Hands-On Model Building

Workshops where students design and build model planes, learning engineering fundamentals.

Mentorship & Exploration

Meet real engineers and pilots, explore career paths, and ask questions during live sessions.

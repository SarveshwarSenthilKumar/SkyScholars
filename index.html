Founded by Sarveshwar Senthil Kumar and Kamryn Walker, SkyScholars is dedicated to introducing middle schoolers to the exciting world of aeronautical engineering.

About Us

SkyScholars empowers the next generation of engineers through engaging workshops, hands-on experiments, and mentorship opportunities. We inspire students to explore the science of flight while fostering creativity, problem-solving, and a passion for STEM.

Our Programs

Aerodynamics Basics

Interactive lessons and demos explaining lift, drag, thrust, and flight control principles.

Hands-On Model Building

Workshops where students design and build model planes, learning engineering fundamentals.

Mentorship & Exploration

Meet real engineers and pilots, explore career paths, and ask questions during live sessions.

Meet the Founders

Sarveshwar Senthil Kumar

Sarveshwar is a passionate STEM advocate, programmer, and builder who co-founded SkyScholars to make aeronautical engineering accessible and exciting for young students. He believes in learning by doing and bringing big ideas to life. You can view some his previous projects on his portfolio website at SarveshwarSenthilKumar.github.io

Kamryn Walker

Kamryn is an aspiring aerospace engineer with a deep interest in education and youth mentorship. Through SkyScholars, he helps students explore STEM and discover their potential in creative and inspiring ways.
